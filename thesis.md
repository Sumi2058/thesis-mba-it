# Thesis Overview

## Thesis Title

**Risk Perception and Post-Purchase Problems in Online Shopping in Nepal: A Study Integrating Payment Issues, Scams, and Product Quality Mismatch**

---

## Research Objectives

1. To identify common payment problems faced by online shoppers in Nepal.
2. To study how online scams affect customer trust and behaviour.
3. To understand issues related to product quality mismatch in online shopping.
4. To find how these factors affect customers' behaviour after purchase.
5. To find the relationship between risk perception and customer satisfaction in online shopping.

---

## Research Variables

| Role | Variable(s) |
|---|---|
| **Dependent variable** | Post-purchase behaviour |
| **Independent variables** | Payment issues, Scams, Product quality mismatch |
| **Mediating variable** | Risk perception |
